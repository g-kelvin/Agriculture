About Us

Quality Champion consultancy(QCC) is agricultural consultancy firm offering services to agricultural industry.We do third party audit for GlobalG.A.P,SMETA and Rainforest alliance and utz .We also help SME who can not afford to hire quality compliance staff in preparing there farm for internal audits especially Global G.A.P .

We source agricultural experts for other companies. The companies looking for agronomist,supervisor and advisors including quality assurance staffs. We are committed to entrench the culture of quality to our society.QCC believes in quality work that meets the customer requirement and brings satisfaction to end user of our products and services.

We are currently offering services to East Africa countries Burudi,Rwanda,Tanzania ,Uganda and Kenya.The main office is in Westlands Nairobi.

VISION
To be a leading and vibrant centre of excellence in sustainable agriculture, food, nutrition and environmental sciences and stewardship.

MISSION

To unlock the potential of nature to improve the quality of life.
